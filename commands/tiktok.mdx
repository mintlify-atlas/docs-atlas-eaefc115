The `/ads tiktok` command runs a full TikTok Ads audit centered on the platform's creative-first model. Unlike Google or Meta where targeting and bidding drive most of the performance differential, TikTok success depends primarily on creative quality—native-style content, fast hooks, sufficient creative volume, and sound-on design. The audit evaluates 28 checks across five weighted categories and produces a `TIKTOK-ADS-REPORT.md` with a health score, creative scorecard, Smart+ vs manual performance comparison, and TikTok Shop readiness assessment.

## Scope

`/ads tiktok` covers all major TikTok campaign types:

- Standard In-Feed Ads (prospecting and retargeting)
- Spark Ads (organic content whitelisted for paid promotion)
- Smart+ campaigns (TikTok's automated campaign type)
- GMV Max campaigns (mandatory for TikTok Shop Ads since July 2025)
- Video Shopping Ads and LIVE Shopping Ads
- Search Ads (via Search Ads Toggle)
- Pangle network placements

## Category breakdown and weights

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Creative Quality — 30%">
    **12 checks: T05–T10, T20–T25**

    Creative is the #1 success factor on TikTok. The platform's algorithm uses creative signals for targeting in a similar way to Meta's Andromeda—content that performs well organically tends to perform well as an ad.

    | Check | Description | Severity |
    |-------|-------------|----------|
    | T05 | ≥6 creative assets per ad group | **Critical** |
    | T06 | All video assets 9:16 vertical (1080×1920) | **Critical** |
    | T07 | Native-looking content, not corporate/polished | High |
    | T08 | Hook in first 1–2 seconds | High |
    | T09 | No creative active >7 days with declining CTR | High |
    | T10 | Spark Ads tested (+142% engagement rate, +43% CVR vs standard In-Feed) | High |
    | T20 | TikTok Shop catalog connected (e-commerce) | Medium |
    | T21 | Video Shopping Ads tested for catalog accounts | Medium |
    | T22 | Captions include high-intent keywords for search discovery | High |
    | T23 | Trending or engaging audio used (sound-on platform) | Medium |
    | T24 | Appropriate CTA button selected, not default | Medium |
    | T25 | Key content within safe zone (X:40–940, Y:150–1470) | High |

    **The creative-first rules:**
    - Sound-on is non-negotiable: TikTok is consumed with sound 93% of the time. Silent video ads are a Quality Gate failure.
    - Vertical-only: 9:16 is the only accepted format. Letterboxed horizontal content fails T06.
    - ≥6 creatives per ad group: below this threshold, the algorithm cannot test effectively and the ad group cannot exit the learning phase on creative signals alone.
    - Refresh every 7–10 days: TikTok's average creative lifespan is 7–10 days. High-spend accounts ($1K+/day) need fresh variations every 3–4 days.

    <Warning>
      Campaigns with 10+ unique creatives achieve 1.3× higher ad recall, 3.0× purchase intent, and 1.5× higher awareness compared to campaigns with fewer than 5 creatives.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Technical Setup — 25%">
    **2 checks: T01–T02**

    TikTok has a unique technical requirement: the `ttclid` (TikTok Click ID) must be captured, stored, and sent back with every conversion event. Without it, attribution breaks for a significant portion of conversions.

    | Check | Description | Severity |
    |-------|-------------|----------|
    | T01 | TikTok Pixel installed and firing on all pages | **Critical** |
    | T02 | Events API + ttclid passback active | High |

    **ttclid requirements:**
    1. Captured on first page load from the URL parameter
    2. Stored in session storage or a cookie
    3. Sent back with ALL conversion events via the Events API

    Without ttclid passback, TikTok falls back to modeled attribution, which over-claims conversions and makes optimization decisions unreliable.

    The Events API Gateway (v1.5 check T-EA1) simplifies server-side setup and recovers 13%+ of missed conversions compared to pixel-only tracking. SKAN 4 integration with coarse conversion values is required for iOS.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Bidding & Learning — 20%">
    **3 checks: T11–T13**

    TikTok's learning phase requires 50 conversions per 7 days per ad group to stabilize. Underfunded ad groups never exit learning and never optimize. Budget sufficiency is checked first because it gates everything else.

    | Check | Description | Severity |
    |-------|-------------|----------|
    | T11 | Bid strategy matches goal (Lowest Cost for volume, Cost Cap for efficiency) | High |
    | T12 | Daily budget ≥50× target CPA per ad group | High |
    | T13 | Ad group achieving ≥50 conversions/week (learning phase exit criterion) | High |

    **Budget sufficiency minimums:**

    | Level | Minimum |
    |-------|---------|
    | Campaign | $50/day |
    | Ad group | $20/day |
    | Recommended | ≥50× target CPA/day per ad group |

    **Learning phase rules:**
    - Never make edits during active learning (resets the phase)
    - Never increase budget by more than 20% at a time
    - 3× Kill Rule: pause ad groups with CPA >3× target

    For GMV Max campaigns (TikTok Shop): budget must be ≥50× target CPA. Total Shop performance is evaluated as GMV / (ad cost + affiliate commission), not just direct ROAS.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Structure & Settings — 15%">
    **5 checks: T03–T04, T14–T16**

    Campaign structure and the Search Ads Toggle have the largest structural impact on performance. Search Ads can add 20% more conversions when enabled alongside In-Feed campaigns.

    | Check | Description | Severity |
    |-------|-------------|----------|
    | T03 | Separate campaigns for prospecting vs retargeting | High |
    | T04 | Smart+ campaigns tested (42% adoption, 1.41–1.67 median ROAS) | Medium |
    | T14 | Search Ads Toggle enabled (20% conversion uplift combined with In-Feed; 57% of TikTok users use search) | High |
    | T15 | Placement selection reviewed (TikTok, Pangle, etc.) | Medium |
    | T16 | Dayparting aligned with target audience activity | Low |

    **Search Ads Toggle**: 57% of TikTok users actively search the platform. Enabling Search Ads alongside In-Feed campaigns means 18% of users who didn't convert via In-Feed will convert via Search Ads—representing a clean incremental conversion layer. Supports up to 1,000 keywords per ad group with exact, phrase, and broad match types plus negative keywords and search term reports.

    **Smart+ modular control (2025)**: Advertisers can now lock specific modules—targeting, creative, budget, or placement—while automating others. This addresses the previous all-or-nothing limitation. Supports up to 30 ad groups per campaign, 30 asset groups per ad group, and 50 creatives per asset group.
  </Tab>

## TikTok safe zone

All critical text, logos, and CTAs must be within the safe zone:

```
┌──────────────────────────────┐
│  0–150px: Status bar          │  ← TOP UNSAFE
├──────────────────────────────┤
│                         │    │
│    SAFE ZONE            │140 │  ← RIGHT: Like, comment,
│    X: 40–940px          │ px │     share, profile icons
│    Y: 150–1470px        │    │
│    (900×1320px)         │    │
│                         │    │
├──────────────────────────────┤
│  Caption, music, CTA, nav    │  ← BOTTOM UNSAFE
└──────────────────────────────┘
```

Content placed in the top 150px, right 140px, or bottom 450px will be covered by TikTok's native UI elements. Safe zone compliance (T25) is a High severity check.

## Spark Ads assessment

Spark Ads whitelist organic TikTok posts for use as paid ads, preserving all organic engagement and comments. The audit evaluates:

- Are Spark Ads being used at all? (If not, High priority recommendation)
- What is the CTR of Spark Ads vs standard In-Feed Ads?
- Are winning organic posts being identified and converted to Spark Ads at scale?

Spark Ads benchmarks vs standard In-Feed:
- +30% completion rate
- +142% engagement rate
- +43% CVR
- −4% CPM
- Profile landing page variant: 69% higher CVR, 37% lower CPA

## GMV Max and TikTok Shop

GMV Max became mandatory for all TikTok Shop campaigns in July 2025. The audit evaluates:

- All Shop Ads campaigns using GMV Max (v1.5 check T-GM1; non-compliance is Critical)
- Product feed quality and completeness
- Video Shopping Ads, LIVE Shopping Ads, and Product Shopping Ads present
- Shop CVR benchmark: >10% (vs 0.46–2.4% for standard landing pages)
- Affiliate program configured

## Key thresholds

| Metric | Pass | Warning | Fail |
|--------|------|---------|------|
| CTR (in-feed) | ≥1.0% | 0.5–1.0% | &lt;0.5% |
| Creatives per ad group | ≥6 | 3–5 | &lt;3 |
| Video watch time | ≥6s | 3–6s | &lt;3s |
| Learning conversions/week | ≥50 | 30–50 | &lt;30 |
| Daily budget | ≥50× CPA | 20–49× CPA | &lt;20× CPA |
| Creative age (declining CTR) | &lt;7 days | 7–14 days | >14 days |
