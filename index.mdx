## What Claude Ads does

Claude Ads analyzes data you export from your ad platforms—spreadsheets, screenshots, or pasted metrics—and returns a structured **Ads Health Score (0–100)** with prioritized findings. It does not connect to any ad platform automatically; you stay in control of your data.

## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Claude Ads">
    Add the plugin in Claude Code with two commands, or use the one-line installer for your OS. See [Installation](/installation) for all options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export your ad account data">
    Download a 30-day export from your ad platform dashboard, or prepare screenshots. Claude Ads works with whatever data you have.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run your first audit">
    Open Claude Code and run `/ads audit` for a full multi-platform review, or target a specific platform like `/ads google` or `/ads meta`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Claude Ads runs entirely on your local machine via Claude Code. No ad account data is sent to external servers. All analysis is private by default.
</Note>
