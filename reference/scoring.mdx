Every audit Claude Ads runs ends with a single number: the Ads Health Score. That number is not an average of pass/fail checks — it is a weighted sum that gives more weight to the checks that affect your revenue most directly. This page explains the exact formula, the per-platform category weights that drive it, and how individual check results translate into a final grade.

## The weighted scoring formula

```text
S_total = Σ(C_pass × W_sev × W_cat) / Σ(C_total × W_sev × W_cat) × 100
```

| Variable | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| `C_pass` | 1 if the check passed, 0.5 if WARNING, 0 if FAIL |
| `W_sev` | Severity multiplier of the individual check |
| `W_cat` | Category weight for that platform |

The result is a 0–100 score. Because each check is weighted by both its severity and the importance of its category, a single critical failure in conversion tracking can drop your score far more than ten low-severity warnings combined.

## Severity multipliers

| Severity | Multiplier | Criteria |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Critical | 5.0 | Immediate revenue or data loss risk. Remediation urgent. |
| High | 3.0 | Significant performance drag. Fix within 7 days. |
| Medium | 1.5 | Optimization opportunity. Fix within 30 days. |
| Low | 0.5 | Best practice, minor impact. Nice to have. |

## Check result values

| Result | Points earned |
|--------|--------------|
| PASS | Full `W_sev × W_cat` |
| WARNING | 50% of full points |
| FAIL | 0 points |
| N/A | Excluded from denominator entirely |

## Grading thresholds

The bands are intentionally wider than academic-style scoring because ad account health is typically distributed lower. A score of 75 or above represents a genuinely well-managed account.

| Grade | Score | Label | Action required |
|-------|-------|-------|-----------------|
| A | 90–100 | Excellent | Minor optimizations only |
| B | 75–89 | Good | Some improvement opportunities |
| C | 60–74 | Needs improvement | Notable issues need attention |
| D | 40–59 | Poor | Significant problems present |
| F | &lt;40 | Critical | Urgent intervention required |

## Cross-platform aggregate score

When auditing multiple platforms, Claude Ads calculates each platform score separately, then combines them weighted by budget share:

```text
Aggregate Score = Σ(Platform_Score × Platform_Budget_Share)
```

**Example:**

```text
Google (82) × 40% + Meta (71) × 35% + LinkedIn (90) × 25%
= 32.8 + 24.85 + 22.5
= 80.15 → Grade B
```

Three checks apply across all platforms simultaneously. They are scored at 100% weight in the aggregate score — not within any single platform.

## Quick Wins logic

Quick Wins are the subset of failing checks worth fixing first — high severity but low remediation effort:

```text
IF severity == "Critical" OR severity == "High"
AND estimated_remediation_time < 15 minutes
THEN flag as "Quick Win"

PRIORITY: Quick Wins sorted by (severity × estimated_impact) DESC
```

**Examples of Quick Wins:**

| Fix | Severity | Est. time |
|-----|----------|-----------|
| Enable Enhanced Conversions | Critical | 5 min |
| Turn on Search Ads Toggle (TikTok) | High | 2 min |
| Add negative keyword lists | Critical | 10 min |
| Fix location targeting method | Critical | 2 min |
| Enable Advantage+ Placements | Medium | 2 min |
